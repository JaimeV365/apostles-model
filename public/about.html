What is the Apostles Model?

The Apostles Model is a customer segmentation framework that analyses customers based on two key dimensions:

  • Satisfaction: How happy customers are with your products or services
  • Loyalty: How likely they are to continue doing business with you

This creates four distinct customer segments:

üëë Apostles

Highly satisfied and highly loyal. Your best customers who love your products and stick with you.

‚≠ê Loyalists

Moderately satisfied but highly loyal. They stick with you but there's room for improvement.

üö® Terrorists
