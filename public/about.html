The Science Behind the Segments

segmentor.app maps customers across two critical dimensions—creating four distinct strategic segments, each requiring a unique approach.

Loyalists

High Satisfaction + High Loyalty

Your growth engine. They love you, they're staying, and they're telling everyone. Nurture these relationships relentlessly.

Mercenaries

High Satisfaction + Low Loyalty

They like your product but won't hesitate to switch for a better deal. Build bonds and emotional connection.

Hostages

Low Satisfaction + High Loyalty

Stuck with you (contracts, switching costs, lack of alternatives). Fix their pain points or risk explosive churn later.

Defectors

Low Satisfaction + Low Loyalty

Already mentally gone. Understand why to prevent future defections. Sometimes, letting go is the strategic choice.
