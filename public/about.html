What is the Apostles Model?

The Apostles Model is a customer segmentation framework that analyses customers based on two key dimensions:

  • Satisfaction: How happy customers are with your products or services
  • Loyalty: How likely they are to continue doing business with you

This creates four distinct customer segments:

üëë Loyalists

Highly satisfied and highly loyal. Your best customers who love your products and stick with you. Includes the Advocates sub-zone.

üí∞ Mercenaries

Highly satisfied but not loyal. They like your product but will switch for better deals or features.

üîí Hostages

Loyal but not satisfied. They stick with you but aren't happy - they might leave soon.

‚ö†Ô∏è Defectors

Neither satisfied nor loyal. These customers might spread negative word-of-mouth. Includes the Trolls sub-zone.
